## Implementation Goal

Add a new Phase 1 sector system where:

- Interns, Junior Traders, and Senior Traders still produce by default on the General Desk
- players can assign those human units into sectors for multiplier-based output

- the new system is fully integrated into state, selectors, save/load, economy, and stats
